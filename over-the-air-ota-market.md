<p><p>Over the Air (OTA) refers to the wireless distribution of software updates, enhancements, or fixes to devices. The Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) market focuses on updating the low-level software that controls hardware components, ensuring optimal performance and security. In contrast, the Software Over-The-Air (SOTA) market deals with higher-level applications and system software updates, enhancing user experience and functionality. Both markets are essential for maintaining device efficiency, ensuring security, and providing users with the latest features seamlessly.</p></p>
