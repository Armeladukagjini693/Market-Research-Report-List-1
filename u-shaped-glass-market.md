<p><p>U-shaped glass is categorized into two primary types based on light transmission properties: transparent and translucent. Transparent U-shaped glass allows for clear visibility and maximum light passage, making it ideal for applications where unobstructed views are essential, such as facades and windows. In contrast, translucent U-shaped glass diffuses light, offering privacy while still allowing natural illumination, making it suitable for spaces like partitions and skylights. Both types serve distinct architectural and functional roles in modern design.</p></p>
