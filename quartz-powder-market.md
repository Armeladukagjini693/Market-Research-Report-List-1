<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quartz powder market can be categorized into two main types: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing quartz powder into specific shapes using heat and pressure, resulting in strong and durable products ideal for various applications. In contrast, casting molding entails pouring a liquid mixture of quartz powder into molds, allowing it to set into intricate shapes or designs. Both methods leverage the unique properties of quartz powder to produce materials used in industries like construction, electronics, and ceramics.</p></p>
