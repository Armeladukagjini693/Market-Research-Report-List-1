<p><p>The micro and mini sprinklers market is categorized into two main types based on their positioning: upright and upside down. Upright sprinklers are designed to spray water in a downward direction, effectively covering a specific area while minimizing soil erosion. In contrast, upside down sprinklers are installed inverted, allowing water to disperse from a higher point, often leading to broader coverage and reduced wind effects. Both types are essential for efficient water distribution in agriculture and landscaping.</p></p>
