<p><strong>The CMOS Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS industrial cameras market comprises two main types: area scan and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture full 2D images of a scene in a single frame, making them ideal for applications like surface inspection and object recognition. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is advantageous for inspecting moving objects or continuous surfaces, such as in web inspection or large-format scanning. Both types are essential for automation and quality control in various industries.</p></p>
