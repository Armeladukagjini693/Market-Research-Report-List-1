<p><p>The fixed-mount barcode readers market consists of three main types: laser scanners, linear imagers, and 2D imagers. Laser scanners utilize laser beams to read barcodes quickly and accurately, making them ideal for high-speed environments. Linear imagers capture 1D barcodes using light sensors, providing reliable scanning for traditional applications. In contrast, 2D imagers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, offering versatility and higher data capture. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various industries, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in barcode scanning.</p></p>
