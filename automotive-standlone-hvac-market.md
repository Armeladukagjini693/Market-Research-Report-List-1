<p><p>The automotive standalone HVAC market comprises two main types: manual HVAC and automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC systems require drivers to adjust temperature and airflow settings manually, providing a basic control level. In contrast, automatic HVAC systems utilize sensors and algorithms to regulate cabin conditions autonomously, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency. Automatic systems respond to changes in temperature and humidity, offering a more sophisticated and user-friendly experience, which is driving their increasing adoption in modern vehicles.</p></p>
