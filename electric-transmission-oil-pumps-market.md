<p><p>The Electric Transmission Oil Pumps market is categorized into two main types: Integrated Pumps and Separate Pumps. Integrated Pumps are designed as a single unit combined with the transmission system, providing compactness and ease of installation. They effectively manage oil circulation within the transmission. In contrast, Separate Pumps operate independently, allowing for flexibility in placement and maintenance. These pumps are often used in larger systems where dedicated oil management is necessary, ensuring optimal performance and reliability of the transmission.</p></p>
