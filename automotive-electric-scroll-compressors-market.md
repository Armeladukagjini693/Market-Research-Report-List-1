<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Scroll Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hermetic Scroll Compressors</li><li>Semi-hermetic Scroll Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric scroll compressors market comprises two primary types: hermetic and semi-hermetic scroll compressors. Hermetic scroll compressors feature a sealed design that prevents refrigerant leaks, housing the compressor motor and components within a single unit, enhancing efficiency and reducing noise. In contrast, semi-hermetic scroll compressors have a removable cover for maintenance purposes, allowing for repairs without replacing the entire unit. Both types are essential in electric vehicle HVAC systems, providing efficient cooling and climate control while promoting energy savings.</p></p>
