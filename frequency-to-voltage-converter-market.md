<p><ul><li>Analog Channel</li><li>Differential Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Frequency-to-Voltage Converter market is segmented into two main types: Analog Channel and Differential Channel. Analog Channel converters output a continuous voltage proportional to the input frequency, making them suitable for a range of applications where precise frequency measurements are essential. On the other hand, Differential Channel converters utilize two input signals to enhance accuracy and noise rejection, ideal for environments with signal interference. Both types serve critical roles in industrial automation, telecommunications, and measurement systems.</p></p>
