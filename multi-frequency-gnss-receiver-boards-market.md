<p><p>The multi-frequency GNSS receiver boards market is segmented into dual-frequency, tri-frequency, and others. The "others" category includes specialized receivers that might utilize additional frequencies or unique configurations tailored for specific applications, catering to niche markets such as surveying, aviation, and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
